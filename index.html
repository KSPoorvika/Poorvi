Festivals

India celebrates a wide variety of festivals such as Diwali, Holi, Navratri, Eid, Christmas, and Pongal. Each one reflects a blend of cultural heritage and religious significance.

Languages

India is home to 22 official languages and over 1,600 dialects. Some of the major languages include Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Marathi, Bengali, and Gujarati.

Clothing

Indian traditional clothing varies by region. Common attire includes sarees, salwar kameez, lehenga choli for women, and kurta-pajama, sherwani, dhoti for men, often made with rich textiles and embroidery.

Food

Indian cuisine is famous worldwide. Signature dishes include butter chicken, biryani, masala dosa, chaat, and sweets like rasgulla, jalebi, and kulfi. Each region brings its own flavor to the table.
